THE LEGACY

An 18-Year Legacy of Orchestrating Flawless Milestones.

Since 2007, Studio Erish has been the silent force behind some of the most exclusive destination weddings, avant-garde debuts, and high-profile corporate galas across Davao City and the greater Davao Region.

The Art of the Celebration

What began nearly two decades ago as a passionate pursuit of refined hospitality has evolved into a premier full-service event coordination practice. We believe that a true milestone is not merely scheduled; it is meticulously composed. Every texture, every cue, and every transition is designed to evoke emotion and reflect the distinct identity of our patrons.

From intimate oceanfront vows on Samal Island to grand ballroom transformations in the heart of downtown Davao, our signature approach blends rigorous logistical precision with an uncompromising eye for contemporary design.
