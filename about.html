Est. 2007  ·  Davao City, Philippines

My name is Erish — and I have been living and breathing events since 2007, when I stepped into the world of weddings as a coordinator and planner with nothing but a deep passion for creating order out of emotion, and beauty out of vision.

"I did not just stumble into this industry. I was called to it — and nearly two decades later, that calling has never wavered."

What began as a deep love for weddings grew into a full mastery of the craft. Over the years, I have had the privilege of being trusted with hundreds of couples' most sacred days — standing behind the scenes, ensuring that every flower, every timeline, every moment unfolded exactly as it was meant to.

I have seen the tears of a mother watching her daughter walk down the aisle. I have felt the quiet exhale of a groom when he finally sees his bride. I have held the hands of overwhelmed couples and turned their anxiety into anticipation. Those moments are why I do what I do.

Erish Studio was born from that experience — not just as a business, but as a commitment to excellence, intimacy, and artistry in every celebration I touch. Whether it is an intimate ceremony for 50 or a grand affair for hundreds, I bring the same level of dedication, precision, and heart to every single event.

Because to me, your wedding is not just another event on my calendar. It is the most important day of your life — and I treat it exactly that way.
